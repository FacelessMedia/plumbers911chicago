# Plumbers 911 Chicago — Brand Voice Guide

## Voice Summary
Direct. Practical. No-nonsense expertise. We talk like experienced tradespeople who respect your time and your wallet.

## Core Traits

### 1. Direct & Confident
- Lead with the problem, follow with the solution
- "Your water heater is leaking. Here's what that means and what we do about it."
- NOT: "Are you experiencing issues with your water heating system?"

### 2. Practical Knowledge
- Reference real materials, tools, processes
- "We'll camera-inspect your sewer line before we start digging."
- NOT: "Our state-of-the-art technology provides comprehensive diagnostics..."

### 3. Straight Talk on Money
- Give real price ranges with what's included
- "A water heater swap runs $800-$1,500. That covers the unit, fittings, disposal, and labor."
- NOT: "Contact us for a personalized quote!"

### 4. Local & Specific
- Reference Chicago neighborhoods, building types, local codes
- "Pre-war Greystones on the South Side usually have cast iron drains past their prime."

### 5. Trustworthy Without Begging
- State credentials once. Let expertise build trust naturally.
- "This is one of the most common calls we get in winter."

## Tone Rules
- Short punchy sentences, not long-winded paragraphs
- Say "we" not "our team of highly trained professionals"
- Active voice, not passive
- Name specific parts and processes, not "comprehensive solutions"
- Give real price ranges, not "call for pricing"
- Reference Chicago details, not generic city content
- Address the homeowner directly

## Words to Use
fix, repair, replace, install, inspect, pipe, fitting, valve, drain, line, trap, leak, clog, backup, burst, crack, copper, PVC, cast iron, galvanized, PEX, code-compliant, same-day, 24/7, emergency

## Words to Avoid
comprehensive, solutions, leverage, utilize, state-of-the-art, cutting-edge, innovative, satisfaction guaranteed, don't hesitate, "your plumbing needs", free estimate

## Content Structure (Service Pages)
1. **Opening** — 1-2 sentences. Name the problem.
2. **What we do** — Plain English steps.
3. **When you need it** — Signs and symptoms.
4. **What it costs** — Real range, materials + labor included, prices vary disclaimer.
5. **FAQs** — At least 5 real questions people actually search for.
6. **CTA** — One clear next step.
